Before Dawn

Posted on 2024-08-14 In writings

‘ For the shadow ‘
<br>

Once upon a time, there was this little girl named Mona who lives by the dark woods.
A freezing night when the moon was just covered by the thick clouds which indicated the rainstorm at nose, Mona, together with her little brother, Frank, went on a mysterious adventure across the forest.

It was almost midnight when they found themselves lost.
The fierce storm roaring and the towering trees shaking, they began to fear. For a long time mom had been teaching them not to enter the woods after sunset, only they never took her seriously.

If you are gonna make a tent for the night in such a forest, the first thing to do is to make a campfire against the darkness and the chill. Unfortunately, you would have to prepare firewoods beforehand since the forest is so damp that you can’t find a single dry stick even in the sunny days. There are a lot wierd things about the forest which always appears by the name Shadowoods, nevertheless, it’s not the right time for the story teller to open his chatty backpack: two kids are stuck in grave danger, remember?
I mean, to make things worse, it happened that neither of the two had brought firewoods before departure.

It was so dark and freezing and they knew that it would be impossible for them to survive this night without a campfire. But how to make one?
